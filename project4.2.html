12 Months of...

Tool(s): Adobe Stock and Premiere Pro

Project Description: I had to create a minute-long video by compiling twelve separate videos, one for each month. To do this, I searched for the videos on Adobe Stock and merged them together using Premiere Pro. I also added small captions to indicate which month each video represented. This assignment was quite challenging as I had never used Premiere Pro before, so it took me some time to figure out how to use it properly. But eventually, I got the hang of it.
